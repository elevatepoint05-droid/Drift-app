DRIFT PRD v1
Product Name
Drift
Product Vision
Drift is an AI-powered wellness intelligence platform that helps people understand, predict, and optimize their daily energy, focus, and recovery.
Instead of only tracking health metrics, Drift helps users know:
* When they will perform best
* When they should focus
* When they should rest
* How today's habits affect tomorrow's performance
The goal is to transform wellness data into actionable daily decisions.
________________


Problem
Most wellness apps only show data.
Users see:
* Sleep score
* Heart rate
* Recovery score
But they still don't know:
"What should I do today?"
Existing wellness apps provide information but rarely provide clear guidance.
________________


Solution
Drift converts wellness data into daily intelligence.
The app predicts:
* Focus windows
* Energy peaks
* Recovery capacity
* Stress risk
Then provides personalized recommendations.
Example:
"Your highest focus window today is 09:15–11:00."
"Recovery is elevated. Deep work is recommended."
"Energy dip expected at 14:30. Take a short walk."

Core Features
1. Energy Score
Single score representing:
* Energy
* Recovery
* Mental readiness
Scale:
0–100
Updates daily.
________________


2. Daily Forecast
Predictive timeline showing:
* Energy peaks
* Focus windows
* Recovery periods
* Energy dips
Visual forecast throughout the day.
________________


3. Wellness Intelligence
AI-generated insights based on user behavior.
Examples:
"Flow state probability is high this morning."
"Sleep quality increased recovery by 12%."
"Stress accumulation is lower than average."
________________


4. Daily Check-In
Simple emotional check-in:
😀 Great
🙂 Good
😐 Okay
😔 Low Energy
Used to improve AI predictions.
________________


5. Habit Impact Analysis
Shows how habits affect performance.
Examples:
* Sleep
* Exercise
* Water intake
* Screen time
* Caffeine
